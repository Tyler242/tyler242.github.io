Purpose of Outside Yet Weather

Worried if you will get rained on while hiking? Concerned that it will be too cold to go white water rafting? The purpose of On Demand Weather will be to inform its users about the current and future weather patterns and how it will affect whatever outdoor activity they may have planned. We want our users to feel that they can visit our site to gain information about the weather and how it will affect their outdoor adventures. Is it a good day to stay in and watch a movie? Or is it just right for jumping out of an airplane.

Domain Name: outsideyet.com

I choose this domain name because it reflects the purpose of the website and it was available when I found it. The price listed through the google domain search was $12/year.
