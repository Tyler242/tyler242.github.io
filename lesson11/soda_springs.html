Soda Springs Idaho | sodaspringsid.com

Know as the Oregon Trail Oasis, Soda Springs and the surrounding area has a great history. Pioneers headed towards California and Oregon traveled through this area and many stopped because of the abundance of water and natural springs. Soda Springs is also home to the only captive Geyser in the world. Come pay us a visit!
