Fish Haven Idaho | Bear Lake

Fish Haven, Idaho, located on the shore of Bear Lake right near the Idaho-Utah border, is an unincorporated community that draws many to come and grab a bite to eat after a day on the lake. The first settlement was made in 1864 and has since become a favorite fishing spot of the locals and others in the surrounding Idaho and Utah areas. If you're looking for a fun day on the lake or would like to catch some fish, come check out Fish Haven!
